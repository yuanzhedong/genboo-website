Our Story

We've known each other for nearly ten years, working together at top AI labs like NVIDIA, Google, and Baidu Research. Along the way, we've built cutting-edge AI systems, published research, and shipped products used by millions.

But we've always shared a bigger dream: What if we could create the kind of learning experience we wished for as kids?

This isn't just another coding school. It's a passion project from a team that's been at the forefront of AI and robotics, now dedicated to making that same technology accessible and magical for the next generation.
