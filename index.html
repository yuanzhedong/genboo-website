Our Story

Building Together for the Next Generation

We've known each other for nearly ten years, working together at top AI labs and universities like Carnegie Mellon University, Stanford, NVIDIA, Google, Baidu Research, and Apple Special Projects Group (SPG). Along the way, we've built cutting-edge AI systems, published research, and shipped products used by millions.

But we've always shared a bigger dream: What if we could create the kind of learning experience we wished for as kids?

This isn't just another coding school. It's a passion project from a team that's been at the forefront of AI and robotics, now dedicated to making that same technology accessible and magical for the next generation.
